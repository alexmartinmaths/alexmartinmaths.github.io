NBGGT

North British Geometric Group Theory

The NBGGT network is a joint seminar series, meeting three times a year, that brings together mathematicians working in (geometric) group theory and related areas from universities from Scotland and the North of England: Aberdeen, Durham, Edinburgh, Glasgow, Manchester, St Andrews, etc.

This seminar series has been running since 2003, an archive of previous meetings can be found at this link.

To receive updates and announcement of our next meetings, please subscribe to our NBGGT mailing list.

We are very grateful for financial support from the London Mathematical Society and the Glasgow Mathematical Journal Learning and Research Support Fund.
