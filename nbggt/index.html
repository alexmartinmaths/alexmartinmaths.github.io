North British Geometric Group Theory

The NBGGT network is a joint seminar series, meeting three times a year, that brings together mathematicians working in (geometric) group theory and related areas from universities from Scotland and the North of England: Aberdeen, Durham, Edinburgh, Glasgow, Manchester, St Andrews, etc. The network is currently coordinated by Laura Ciobanu and Alexandre Martin.

In 2024-2025, the NBGGT will meet in Glasgow, Durham, and St Andrews.

The NBGGT network has been running since 2003. Details of past meetings can be found at the NBGGT archive.

To receive updates and announcement of our next meetings, please subscribe to our NBGGT mailing list.

We are very grateful for financial support from the London Mathematical Society and the Glasgow Mathematical Journal Learning and Research Support Fund.
